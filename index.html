    About Us

    Welcome to Siliguri Mount Point School, where education meets innovation. Our school is a vibrant hub of knowledge, creativity, and community. With a rich history of academic excellence, we continually embrace modern teaching methods to empower our students for a dynamic world. At SMPS, we foster a culture of curiosity, critical thinking, and compassion, ensuring a well-rounded education that prepares students for success.

    Our Mission

    Empowering future leaders through holistic education. Our mission is to nurture young minds, instill values, and foster a passion for learning. We are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and innovative environment where each student can thrive andis able to give their best.

    Our Plan

    Guided by our mission, our plan is to continually enhance our curriculum, leverage cutting-edge technology, and invest in exceptional faculty. We aim to create a dynamic learning ecosystem that equips students with the skills and knowledge needed for a rapidly evolving world.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to be a beacon of educational excellence, recognized globally for producing responsible, adaptable, and compassionate citizens. We aspire to shape a brighter tomorrow by empowering our students with the tools to lead, innovate, and make a positive impact on society.
